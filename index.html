Who am I personally?

MY HOBBIES SPEAK ABOUT ME A LOT!

ART & FOLKLORE

Deep care about cultural aspects of design. I love all the tribe stories and museums to widen my perspective and understand creation better.

PSYCHOLOGY

My favourite is cognitive psychology to catch all the fun biases of the brain and use them in the marketing aspect of my projects. Yeah, I know nobody reads icon descriptions.

PHOTOGRAPHY

I used to be a part-time photographer for occasions and also created artistic photography. This taught me a lot about the pictures!

DRAWING

In my free time I create fine art, illustrations and paintings. It balances my needs for expressions and helps me in making unique designs.

DRAMA MOVIES

Related to psychology! I love the style like Charlie Kaufman’s - the master of immersion, deep understanding and very unique sense of humour.

DARK MUSIC

I think music reflects our nature. I love atmospheric and natural music within genres like folk, progressive / death / black metal, but also fun like 70’s rock. Want to go with me to a gig?

TEA

Tea has charms to soothe the savage breast! There is no good day without a tasty cup of tea with a buddy.

FOOD

Designing food to be nutritious and beautiful - I think the composition of tastes relates to visual impressions.
